# Sound attribution

Engine audio is **procedurally synthesized** in-game (Web Audio API) to mimic an F1 V6 turbo-hybrid:
high-RPM scream, turbo whistle, V6 firing pulse, and lift-off exhaust crackle.

Legacy Mixkit samples in this folder are unused by the current build but remain for reference:

- `engine-loop-low.wav` — Mixkit (#2732)
- `engine-loop-mid.wav` — Mixkit (#2650)
- `engine-loop-high.wav` — Mixkit (#2704)
- `drive-by.wav` — Mixkit (#1538)
